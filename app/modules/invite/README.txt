Invite is an invitation system that lets you and your site members invite more people to join the site. Invitations are
important to encourage expansion of your network and to provide an exponential growth of community interest. This module
provides an “Invite a Friend” feature that allows your users to send and track invitations.

Installation
============
  * Enable the invite and invite_by_email modules. 
  * Navigate Structure » Invite types » Add Invite type and add an invite type.
  * Navigate Structure » Block layout and place the invite type block that you named.
  * Enter an email address in the block and click Send Invitation.
  
Configuration
=============
  * Navigate Structure » Invite types » Invite settings to check the default configuration.
